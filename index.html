Our story

Wallpaper World was established in 1976 as a small family owned business catering to local residents for all their wallcovering needs.

Over 30 years later Wallpaper World Interiors has become one of Florida's premier decorating centers for both residential and commercial clients.

No matter what the size or scope of your commercial or residential project, it is out personal goal to provide our clients with quality service and products. We have developed long standing relationships with our suppliers in the industry that enables us to furnish you quality products at competitive prices.

What makes us unique ?

Our customer service! We will go the extra mile to provide recommendations for every style and budget. We invite you to visit our beautiful showroom where our knowledgeable and friendly staff will be happy to assist you with any of your design needs.
